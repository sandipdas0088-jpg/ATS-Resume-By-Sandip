🎯 ATS Optimization

Our resume builder is specifically designed to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). Features include:

  • Standard fonts compatible with ATS parsing
  • Simple formatting without complex layouts
  • No graphics or images that ATS can't read
  • Clear section headers for easy parsing
  • Keywords optimization scoring
  • ATS compatibility percentage indicator

📱 Device Compatibility

  • ✅ Mobile Browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Android)

💡 Tips for Best Results

  1. Use clear and descriptive section headers
  2. Keep formatting simple and consistent
  3. Include relevant keywords from job descriptions
  4. List achievements and quantifiable results
  5. Save your resume frequently
  6. Download and test with ATS platforms
